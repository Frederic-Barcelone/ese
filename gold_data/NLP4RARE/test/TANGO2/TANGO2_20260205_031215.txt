TANGO2-related metabolic encephalopathy and arrhythmias is a rare disorder that was first reported in the medical literature in 2016. According to the TANGO2 Research Foundation, as of May 2018, fewer than 30 affected individuals have been identified with the disorder worldwide. Rare diseases often go undiagnosed or misdiagnosed, making it difficult to determine the true frequency in the general population.
